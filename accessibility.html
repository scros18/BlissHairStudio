Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Our Commitment

BlissHairStudio is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Website Accessibility

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. Our website includes:

  • Semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Clear, readable fonts
  • Responsive design for all devices

Salon Accessibility

Our physical location offers:

  • Wheelchair accessible entrance
  • Accessible restroom facilities
  • Adjustable salon chairs
  • Accommodations for service animals
  • Large print menus available upon request

Accessibility Features

  • Screen Readers: Compatible with JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full site navigation without a mouse
  • Text Sizing: Browser zoom supported up to 200%
  • Focus Indicators: Clear visual focus for interactive elements

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to improve:

  • Third-party embedded content accessibility
  • Historical images without alt text
  • Complex interactive elements
